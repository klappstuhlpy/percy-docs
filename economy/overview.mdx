Percy's economy gives every member of your server a wallet they can earn, spend, save, and wager. Coins live on a per-server basis, so your balance in one server is completely separate from any other. There are two places your coins can sit: **cash** (your wallet, spendable anywhere) and the **bank** (safe storage that can't be lost to games or transfers until you withdraw it). Moving money between the two is instant and free.

## Wallet vs. Bank

| Location | What it's for | Can be spent directly? |
|----------|---------------|------------------------|
| **Cash** | Everyday spending, games, the shop, transfers | Yes |
| **Bank** | Safe long-term storage | No — withdraw first |

Deposit cash into the bank to keep it safe, and withdraw when you need to spend. Games and the shop always draw from your **cash** balance, so make sure you've withdrawn before you play.

## Core Commands

| Command | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| `/balance` | Show your cash, bank, and total — or check another member with `/balance @User` |
| `/balance leaderboard` | See the richest members on the server |
| `/deposit <amount>` | Move cash into your bank |
| `/withdraw <amount>` | Pull coins out of your bank into cash |
| `/transfer @User <amount>` | Send cash directly to another member |
| `/daily` | Claim your daily reward and build a streak |

```
/balance
/deposit all
/withdraw 500
/transfer @User 200
/daily
```

<Note>
  `/deposit all` accepts the keyword `all` to move your entire cash balance into the bank at once.
</Note>

## Daily Streak Bonus

Claiming your daily reward once every 24 hours builds a **streak**. Each consecutive day adds a bonus on top of the base payout, rewarding members who show up regularly.

| Streak day | Payout |
|------------|--------|
| Day 1 | 250 coins |
| Day 2 | 300 coins |
| Day 3 | 350 coins |
| Day 4 | 400 coins |
| Day 5 | 450 coins |
| Day 6 | 500 coins |
| Day 7+ | 550 coins (maximum) |

The streak bonus caps at day 7, so the highest possible daily payout is **550 coins**. If you miss a day and more than **48 hours** pass since your last claim, your streak resets to 1.

<Tip>
  Set a reminder to claim `/daily` at the same time each day. As long as you claim within the 24–48 hour window after your last claim, your streak keeps growing.
</Tip>

## Earning Activities

Beyond the daily reward, you can earn coins by going fishing or hunting. Both commands pay out a random amount based on a weighted loot table — common catches pay a little, rare ones pay a lot.

| Command | Cooldown | Description |
|---------|----------|-------------|
| `/fish` | 60 seconds | Cast a line for catches ranging from an old boot to a rare pearl |
| `/hunt` | 90 seconds | Head out for higher variance payouts — rabbits to trophy stags |

Hunting has a longer cooldown but higher maximum payouts than fishing. Both activities are boosted by **loot\_boost** items from the shop — see the [Shop](/economy/shop) page for details.

## Admin Commands

Server administrators (members with **Administrator** or **Manage Guild** permission) have access to commands that directly modify member balances.

| Command | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| `/set-money @User <amount> <cash\|bank>` | Set a specific member's cash or bank balance |
| `/add-money-role @Role <amount> <cash\|bank>` | Add coins to every member who holds a given role |
| `/remove-money @User <amount> <cash\|bank>` | Deduct coins from a specific member's balance |

<Note>
  Economy data is stored per-server. A member's balance in your server is not shared with or affected by any other server.
</Note>
